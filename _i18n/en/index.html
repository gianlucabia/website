RaceUp


Race UP is one of the most important Formula Student academic team: since 2006, more than a hundred male and female students of the Patavina University, from Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace, Electronics, Mechatronics, Management, Economics and Communication Sciences, merge expertise and passion in training a successful experience.

University of Padova

"Universa universis patavina libertas" is the motto that has always characterized the University of Padova, recognized for cultural openness and freedom of expression that prompted centuries ago a group of students and teachers to leave the University of Bologna for Padua and shared by the Paduan students today; these factors also converge in Race UP, which helps make the University of Padua a benchmark for excellence in training and in research studies internationally.
