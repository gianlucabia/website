Sponsors that collaborate with RaceUp Electric Division

The Race Up project continues and improves every year thanks to the valuable economic and professional contribution of our group of loyal partners. It is thanks to them that every year Race Up can grow and improve the performance of your car. Our thanks go to

OZ Racing

OZ Racing was born from a passion for wheels. Founded in 1971, it is a legend in the automotive world. OZ in forty years he has collaborated with the most exclusive car manufacturers: Ferrari, Lamborghini, Maserati, Aston Martin and Lotus, McLaren. Collaborations that have resulted in exclusive circles, carefully preserved in the museum at the headquarters in San Martino di Lupari in Padua.
